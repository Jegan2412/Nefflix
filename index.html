Our Story

Born from a kitchen full of heart

The Nefflix started as a dream on a notepad — a little café where every person walking in feels like a regular, every dish feels like a hug, and every rupee spent feels worth it ten times over.

We keep it real: freshly sourced meats, bold marinades, and zero shortcuts. Our non-veg kitchen is a celebration of spice, smoke, and soul — curated personally every single day.
